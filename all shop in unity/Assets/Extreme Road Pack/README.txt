3. -=How to use=-

=Placing=
Simply place the assets from Extreme Road Pack\Models into your scene of choice. 
Al textures and materials are linked. You can apply a different shader or diffuse color, which you can edit in Extreme Road Pack\Models\Model_....\Materials.
